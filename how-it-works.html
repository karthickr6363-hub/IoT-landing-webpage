Simplicity is Power

Deploying advanced IoT infrastructure shouldn't be complicated. Follow our four-step setup to go live in minutes.

01

Plug & Power

Connect your Smart Gateway S1 to powers and your local network router via Ethernet or WiFi. The gateway automatically initializes and checks for the latest security patches.

  • Auto-detection of local network
  • Hardware self-diagnostic
02

Claim Device

Download the IoT.Connect app or log in via the web dashboard. Scan the unique QR code on your gateway to link it to your account instantly.

  • Zero-Config pairing
  • Encrypted owner verification
03

Deploy Nodes

Place your E4 Edge Nodes within the gateway coverage area. They will automatically form a secure mesh network and begin streaming telemetry data.
