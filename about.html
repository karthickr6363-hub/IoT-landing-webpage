Our Mission

To bridge the gap between hardware and intelligence, empowering every business with the power of IoT.

Founded in San Francisco

IoT.Connect started with a simple idea: that managing a fleet of devices should be as easy as checking your email. Since 2021, we've grown from a garage startup to a global platform powering thousands of critical infrastructures.

Our engineers come from diverse backgrounds in silicon design, cloud architecture, and industrial automation.
