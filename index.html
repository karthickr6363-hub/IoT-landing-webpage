Common Questions

Find quick answers to common platform queries.

Is my data stored locally or in the cloud?

Both. Our primary architecture is Edge-First, meaning mission-critical data remains on your local gateway, while analytic summaries are synced to the secure IoT.Connect cloud.

What happens during an internet outage?

All local automation rules (If-this-then-that) continue to execute. Once connection is restored, the gateway automatically performs a delta-sync of all recorded data.

Can I integrate custom hardware?

Absolutely. Our Open-Link API supports MQTT, CoAP, and HTTP protocols, allowing you to connect legacy sensors or custom silicon to our dashboard.
