Common Questions

How many devices can I connect to one gateway?

The S1 Gateway supports up to 2,000 direct connections and over 10,000 in a mesh network.

Does the platform work without internet?

Yes, our edge computing architecture allows local automation to continue even if the cloud connection is lost.

What is the battery life of the E4 Edge Node?

Typical battery life is 2-3 years depending on the sampling frequency and transmission power settings.
